Resource Augmented Generation (RAG) App AI

This is a Resource Augmented Generation App. Have you ever typed into ChatGPT, Gemini, or another LLM, "Give me a step by step tutorial to complete etc......" and it gives you back information from non credible sources? I was so tired of this kind of stuff and paying $20 or more a month to do this so I built this to give me what I need. You upload books and documents in .txt format and my app will locate and give you a research report ready accurate information. For example; studying for cyber certifications, I uploaded 10 books and got a study plan which enabled me to pass the tests with high scores.
